Dance Intervarsities

Date 6/3/2019 Location National Stadium

Dance Intervarsities is an annual dance competition event between various colleges around Ireland. The event is hosted by a different college each year and this year TU Dublin City Campus are delighted to welcome you through our doors! Inters sees hundreds of student dancers perform in styles including Irish, Jazz, Contemporary, Hiphop and Mixed Piece in the hope of obtaining that coveted gold trophy! We hope all of the dancers hard work pays off and TU Dublin wishes them all the best of luck at Intervarsities 2019.
